What is the structure of your dataset?¶

There are approximately 2.29M shared bike rides in the dataset from June 2018 through May 2019 in bay area. The attributes included the trip duration, start/end time, start/end location, and user information such as gender, user type and user age .

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I'm most interested in figuring out when the shared bikes are high in demand. Also I want to explore what is difference in demand between different age, user type and gender.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I expect that start time information (month/weekday/hour) and user data(age/type/gender) will have the strongest effect on utilizations of shared bikes.
